About Toolhearth

Free tools. No sign-up. No friction.

Toolhearth is a free collection of online tools, calculators, converters, and utilities built for everyday use. Every tool on this site works instantly in your browser — no account required, no software to install, no data sent to a server.

What we build

We focus on tools people actually use: unit converters, financial calculators, text formatters, developer utilities, health calculators, and more. If you find yourself opening a spreadsheet or Googling a formula repeatedly, there's probably a Toolhearth page for it.

How it's free

Toolhearth is supported by affiliate partnerships and occasional display advertising. We only recommend products and services we believe are genuinely useful. Affiliate links are disclosed where relevant.

Privacy first

All calculations happen in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the data you enter into our tools. See our Privacy Policy for details.

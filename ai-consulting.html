FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know but were afraid to ask.

How long does a typical engagement take?
A full Audit takes 2 weeks. A Strategy Session runs 3–4 weeks. Full Implementation varies by scope — most projects are 6–12 weeks from kickoff to handoff.
Do I need existing data or a technical team?
Not at all. Part of our Audit is assessing your data readiness. We'll tell you what's missing and help you get there. And we handle the technical build so your team doesn't have to.
What if I just want advice, not a full build?
That's exactly what the AI Audit ($500) and Strategy Session ($1,500) are for. We'll give you a clear, actionable report — implement it yourself or bring us back for the build later.
How do you handle data privacy and compliance?
We take this seriously. All engagements include a compliance review. We're experienced with HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR, and CCPA requirements. Your data stays yours — always.
What's the best package for a small business?
Most small businesses start with the AI Audit ($500). It gives you a high-signal, low-risk picture of where AI will actually move the needle for your specific business.
Do you work with startups or only established companies?
Both. We've worked with 2-person founder teams and 500-person enterprises. We tailor the scope to your size and budget. We also offer discounted rates for early-stage startups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LeadSync AI.

How does the AI find leads?
LeadSync combines dozens of public and premium data sources — company databases, job boards, intent data providers, social networks, and news feeds — to identify companies and contacts that match your ideal customer profile. The AI cross-references and deduplicates every record before it reaches your pipeline.
How accurate are the email addresses and phone numbers?
We verify every email and phone in real time using multi-stage checks (SMTP validation, syntax, domain reputation, and bounce history). Our accuracy consistently exceeds 95% for emails and 85% for phone numbers.
Can I integrate with my existing CRM?
Absolutely. LeadSync offers native one-click integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and Close. We also provide a REST API and Zapier connection for any other tool in your stack.
What are intent signals, and how do they work?
Intent signals track behavioral data — job changes, content consumption, funding announcements, and competitor research — to identify prospects actively in-market for your solution. Growth and Scale plans surface these as priority alerts so you reach buyers at the right moment.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to the features in your chosen tier. No credit card required to start — cancel anytime during the trial with no obligation.
What kind of support do you offer?
Starter plans include email support with a 24-hour response SLA. Growth plans get priority support (4-hour response). Scale plans include a dedicated account manager and same-day support. All plans have access to our knowledge base and community forum.

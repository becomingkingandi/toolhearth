About Sleep Cycles

Sleep cycles are ~90 minuteseach. Waking at the end of a cycleleaves you refreshed. Waking mid-cyclecauses grogginess (sleep inertia).

For best results, aim for 5 or 6 full cycles— that's about 7.5–9 hours. Even 4 cycles (6 hours) works if your schedule is tight.
